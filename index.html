About

I am Akashdeep -- an engineer who loves turning complex problems into simple, scalable, and real-world solutions. I completed my master's in engineering management from Purdue and my bachelor's in mechanical engineering from VIT, India .

My work focuses on leading cross-functional programs across manufacturing engineering, supply chain, and NPI, owning projects from planning through execution to deliver measurable improvements in quality, waste reduction, and operational efficiency.

I enjoy working with cross-functional teams, building prototypes, improving processes, and making sure ideas actually ship from concept to production. I'm driven by data, clarity, and impact -- if something can be measured, it can be improved.

Beyond engineering, I'm passionate about innovation, AI platforms, and building systems that help both people and products perform at their best. I'm always exploring new technologies, new ideas, new challenges and new ways to engineer meaningful impact.
